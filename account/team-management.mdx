## Invite team members

Collaborate with your team by inviting members to your account.

## Add a team member

1. Go to **Settings** → **Team**
2. Click **Invite Member**
3. Enter their email address
4. Select their role:
   - **Admin**: Full access
   - **Editor**: Can edit projects
   - **Viewer**: Read-only access
5. Click **Send Invitation**

## Manage permissions

1. Go to **Settings** → **Team**
2. Click on a team member
3. Update their role or permissions
4. Click **Save**

## Remove a team member

1. Go to **Settings** → **Team**
2. Click on the team member
3. Click **Remove from Team**
4. Confirm the removal
