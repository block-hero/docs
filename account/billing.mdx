## Update your payment method

Keep your account active by ensuring your payment method is up to date.

## Change payment method

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing**
2. Click **Update** next to your current payment method
3. Enter your new card details
4. Click **Save**

## View billing history

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing**
2. Scroll to **Billing History**
3. Click on any invoice to download a PDF

## Update billing address

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing**
2. Click **Edit** next to your billing address
3. Update your information
4. Click **Save**
