## Fix login issues

If you're having trouble signing in, try these solutions.

## Reset your password

If you forgot your password:

1. Go to the login page
2. Click **Forgot password**
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your inbox for the reset link
5. Click the link and create a new password

## Clear browser cache

Old cached data can cause login problems:

1. Clear your browser cache
2. Close and reopen your browser
3. Try logging in again

## Check account status

If your account is suspended or deactivated, you'll see an error when logging in. Contact support if you believe this is a mistake.
