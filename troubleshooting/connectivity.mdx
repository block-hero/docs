## Check your internet connection

If you're experiencing connectivity issues, start by checking your internet connection.

## Troubleshoot connection issues

### Step 1: Test your connection

1. Try accessing other websites
2. If other sites work, the issue may be specific to Block Hero
3. If no sites work, check your internet connection

### Step 2: Clear browser cache

1. Open your browser settings
2. Clear browsing data
3. Select "Cached images and files"
4. Click **Clear data**

### Step 3: Try a different browser

Sometimes browser-specific issues can cause problems. Try accessing Block Hero in:
- Chrome
- Firefox
- Safari
- Edge

## Still having issues?

Contact our support team with details about:
- Your browser and version
- Any error messages you see
- When the issue started
