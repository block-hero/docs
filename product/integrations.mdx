## Available integrations

Block Hero integrates with popular tools to streamline your workflow.

## Connect an integration

1. Go to **Settings** → **Integrations**
2. Browse available integrations
3. Click **Connect** on the integration you want
4. Follow the authentication steps
5. Your integration is now connected!

## Manage integrations

- **View connected integrations**: Go to **Settings** → **Integrations**
- **Disconnect an integration**: Click **Disconnect** next to the integration
- **Configure settings**: Click **Settings** next to the integration
